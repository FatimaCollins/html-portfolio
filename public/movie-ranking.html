The Top Three Movies Every Made

by Fatima Collins

My Top 3 Movies of All Time.

The Color Purple

I love the characters and hilarious scenes that I still mimic til this day!

Interview with a Vampire

I love how close this film was made to the actual characters of the book and stories told. Sometimes movies like to make up their own stuff, "to make it look interesting and then they ruin it!

The Passion of Christ

This movie made my top 3, because it was brillantly made! Mel Gibson did a wonderful job as director with this film, although unlike the other movies that I have watched multiple times. I could only watch this once, because it made me cry and very angry!
